Welcome to our school website.

Abraham Marthoma Memorial Higher Secondary School (AMMHSS), is a higher secondary school situated in Edayaranmula, Pathanamthitta, Kerala, India.

At Our School, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing environment for students to learn, grow, and excel.
